Recipe for Spaghetti

The following recipe will describe how to make delicious spaghetti.
I love to make spaghetti because it is easy to make a lot of it.
Sometimes I even like to add meatballs to it, or horse meat.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Pasta (From Italy if possible)
  • Water (Cannot be in Africa for this)
  • A big metal pot
  • Pasta Sauce (Has tomatoes)
  • Salt (League player tears)
  • Meat if applicable (Ground beef or horse meat)
  • Strainer for the pasta

Steps to make this dish:

  1. Get the big metal pot and fill it with water.
  2. Put the pot on the stove and heat it until it boils.
  3. Place your pasta in the boiling water and salt.
    The salt is so that the pasta does not stick together like conjoined twins.
  4. Boil the pasta until it is al dental.
    This means that the pasta will be soft but not too soft and watery.
  5. Strain the pasta and put it back in the pot.
  6. Mix the pasta and pasta sauce into one big blob.
  7. Now you may add your cooked meat or meat balls to the pasta if need be.
